Chapter Two

My Experiences

In this section of my entries I will link my past experiences with my love of Spring, there is a psychological reason why I associate Spring to be a happy and enjoyable time, not only for the reasons that are apparent such as nature, the longer days, the animals that are associated with Spring. There are many of these reasons the normal person would link with Spring, but in the following paragraphs we will go into details about the psychological reasons.

The Psychological aspect

In my life I have had a number of experiences with Spring that have triggered my association with Spring to be a positive one, from and early age, I have great memories of trips to the local park, staying out later with friends and playing sports, and every Spring I found myself to gather more and more memories as the years went on, each year I would spend my time outdoors in the fresh air enjoying activities and getting into a number of sporting activities, mainly athletics where I have competed in a number of competitions, in the winter months I would train indoors in anticipation of being able to train outdoors.

Friends & Family

There's something special about Spring time in relation to your friends and family, people seem to gather more and come together more, in my family and friends have a number of birthdays in Spring time so I have always linked this time with a time to be with friends and family, there is a lot of events that happen so this also adds to the number of reasons that I enjoy this time of year, it really is a fantastic time.
